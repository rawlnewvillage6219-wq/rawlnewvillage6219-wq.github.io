Character Details:

Leon S. Kennedy
U.S. government agent sent to rescue the President's daughter. Skilled with firearms and combat.
Ashley Graham
The President’s daughter, kidnapped by the cult. Leon must protect her during the game.
Ada Wong
Mysterious agent with her own agenda. Helps Leon but is often secretive.
Luis Sera
Former researcher of Los Illuminados, assists Leon with information and equipment.
Osmund Saddler
Main antagonist controlling the Los Illuminados cult, manipulates villagers and monsters.
