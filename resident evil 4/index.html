Welcome to Resident Evil 4

Resident Evil 4 is a 2023 survival horror game developed and published by Capcom. A remake of the 2005 game Resident Evil 4, it follows Leon S. Kennedy, a U.S. government agent, as he attempts to rescue the President's daughter from a mysterious cult in rural Spain.
